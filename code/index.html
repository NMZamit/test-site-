Our Story

About
the Academy

Born from a passion for tennis excellence and a commitment to the next generation of players, Tennis Academy has grown into one of Europe's most respected training environments.

We combine elite sport methodology with a holistic education, ensuring every player who trains here develops not just as an athlete but as a complete individual. Our coaches bring decades of professional experience to guide each student's unique journey.

University School

A unique program that combines elite tennis training with an internationally recognized academic curriculum. Students graduate as both accomplished athletes and well-rounded scholars, ready for the highest level of professional play and life beyond the court.

Partner universities in Europe and North America offer degree programs specifically designed for our student-athletes, providing seamless integration between sport and education.
